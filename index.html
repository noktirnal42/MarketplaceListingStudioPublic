Workflow

Built for sellers who still want the final say.

Import real photos

Keep the workflow grounded in buyer-visible item photos. The app does not create fake product imagery.

Add seller facts

Capture title, category, brand, condition, size, color, material, price, and honest notes in one place.

Generate editable drafts

Create platform-specific drafts for marketplaces such as eBay, Poshmark, and Facebook Marketplace, then review before copying.

Stay in control

Manual export keeps sellers in the loop. SellDraft Studio does not auto-post or scrape private marketplace data.

AI approach

On-device first, review always.

SellDraft Studio is designed around Apple platform technologies. On eligible iOS and macOS 26+ devices, listing generation can use Apple Foundation Models on device. When unavailable, the app falls back to deterministic local drafting so sellers can keep working. Drafts remain editable before use.
