Common Questions

Does SellDraft Studio post listings automatically?

No. The current app prepares editable listing drafts for manual review and export.

Does the app create fake product photos?

No. The workflow is built around real item photos and honest seller-provided facts.

What should I include in a support request?

Include your device model, OS version, app version, a short description of what happened, and the steps to reproduce it. Screenshots are helpful when they do not contain private information.
