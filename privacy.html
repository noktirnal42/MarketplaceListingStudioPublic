SellDraft Studio Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

Overview

SellDraft Studio is a native listing workspace for resale sellers. The app is designed to help you organize item photos, seller-provided item facts, and editable marketplace listing drafts.

Data Stored By The App

The current app stores item workspaces locally on your device. This may include imported item photos, item titles, categories, brands, condition notes, size, color, material, target price, seller notes, generated draft text, and export history.

AI Listing Generation

On eligible iOS and macOS 26+ devices, SellDraft Studio may use Apple Foundation Models for on-device listing draft generation. When Apple Foundation Models are unavailable, the app uses a local deterministic draft generator.

The current generation path does not send photos, notes, or inventory data to an external AI provider.

Data Collection

SellDraft Studio currently does not collect personal data on a developer-operated server. The app currently does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, account creation, location collection, contacts access, or microphone access.

Photos

Imported item photos are used to support your local listing workflow. The app does not generate replacement product photos or intentionally misrepresent the physical item being listed.

Support Requests

If you contact support, you may choose to provide information such as your email address, device model, OS version, screenshots, and a description of the issue. Do not include marketplace credentials, buyer information, addresses, phone numbers, or other sensitive information in public support requests.

Future Features

If future versions add cloud sync, accounts, subscriptions, external AI providers, or analytics, this privacy policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before those features are released.
